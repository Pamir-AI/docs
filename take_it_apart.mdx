title: 'Take Distiller One Apart'
description: 'Step-by-step guide on how to safely remove the internal components of Distiller One without damaging anything'
---

# step 1  
Remove the screws.

<img src="/images/takeaparts/step1.png" alt="Step 1" /> 

# step 2 
Gently press in the camera part to avoid pulling the cable out when you open the case. (DO NOT STAB THE ACTUAL CAMERA, JUST PRESS ON THE SURROUNDING SURFACE)

<img src="/images/takeaparts/step2.png" alt="Step 2" /> 

# step 3 
Open up the case while applying a forward pushing force.

<img src="/images/takeaparts/step3.png" alt="Step 3" />
